What Is Restorderant?

Restorderant is India's most convenient online food ordering site.
It connects people with the best restaurants around them. Delivering in Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Hyderabad and over 100 other cities, Restorderant has the widest reach in India.

2. Choose

Browse hundreds of menus to find the food you like.


3. Pay

Pay fast & secure online or on cash on delivery.


4. Enjoy

Food is prepared & delivered to your door.
